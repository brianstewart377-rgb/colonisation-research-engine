# M-0001 - Construction Points and Facility Tiers

## Status

Candidate mechanic, high confidence from Mega Guide reference.

## Claim

Colonisation facilities are organised into three tiers. Tier 1 facilities require no construction points and generate Tier 2 construction points. Tier 2 facilities require Tier 2 construction points and generate Tier 3 construction points. Tier 3 facilities require Tier 3 construction points and generate no construction points.

Primary Ports are an exception because they generate their applicable construction points but do not require construction points when built as the initial port in a system.

## Source evidence

- EV-0004 - Colonisation Reference Documents Pack
- Mega Guide v2.3.0, Terms and Definitions, page 6

## Planner implication

The planner must distinguish:

- material cost
- construction point requirement
- construction point generation
- proposed-build totals versus currently-buildable totals

The planner must not treat a Raven negative CP balance that includes all proposed builds as proof that the immediate selected build is blocked.

## Wregoe relevance

During the A4 Orbis decision, Raven displayed negative yellow/green totals because the model included proposed builds. The live Architect preview showed the Orbis was buildable with current system construction points.

## Confidence

High as a reference mechanic. Live in-game previews remain authoritative for specific build availability.
