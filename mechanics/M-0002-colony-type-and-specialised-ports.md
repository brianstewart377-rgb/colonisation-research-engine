# M-0002 - Colony-Type and Specialised Ports

## Status

Candidate mechanic, high confidence from Mega Guide reference.

## Claim

Ports fall into colony-type ports and specialised ports.

Colony-type ports have Colony as their economy type and acquire a specific economy based on local body characteristics and link effects.

Colony-type ports include:

- Civilian space outposts
- Commercial space outposts
- Coriolis
- Orbis
- Ocellus
- Civilian planetary outpost
- Tier 3 Planetary Port

Specialised ports are not Colony type. They have a specific inherent economy and do not inherit an economy from local body characteristics, although their market types can still be affected by strong and weak links.

## Source evidence

- EV-0004 - Colonisation Reference Documents Pack
- Mega Guide v2.3.0, Terms and Definitions, page 7

## Planner implication

The planner must not assume all ports behave identically.

For colony-type ports, economy inheritance and local-body/link context are central.

For specialised ports, inherent economy matters, but strong/weak links can still affect visible market types.

## Wregoe relevance

A4 Orbis/Ocellus/Dodec previews show Facility Economy: Colony. The final market should therefore be verified after construction rather than assumed from station class alone.

## Confidence

High as reference mechanic. Final market behaviour must still be checked in-game after build completion.
