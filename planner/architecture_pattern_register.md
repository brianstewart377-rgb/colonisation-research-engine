# Architecture Pattern Register

This register extracts reusable architecture patterns already named or implied in the repository.

Architecture patterns are reusable only when their prerequisites are met.

## AP-0001 - Industrial Anchor plus High Tech Support Body

- Status: Experimental but promising
- Source: `ontology/roles_assets_strategies.md`, `planner/contextual_strategy_register.md`, `experiments/EXP-0001-a4-complementary-high-tech-role.md`
- Objective served: Broaden total construction-material coverage without dismantling an existing strong Industrial anchor.
- Typical roles:
  - anchor body: `Industrial Anchor`
  - secondary body: `High Tech Support`
- Prerequisites:
  - strong Extraction, Refinery, or Industrial coverage already exists elsewhere
  - High Tech-related coverage is the missing or weak asset
  - body slots remain available for complementarity rather than duplication
- Main risk:
  - High Tech may rise without delivering meaningful new commodity coverage
- Related live verification: `LV-0001`

## AP-0002 - Extraction and Refinery Primary Producer

- Status: Confirmed as a pattern concept
- Source: `ontology/roles_assets_strategies.md`
- Objective served: Concentrate metals-oriented and processing-oriented production on a primary body.
- Typical roles:
  - `Primary Metals Producer`
  - `Primary Refinery Producer`
- Prerequisites:
  - the target body can sustain the necessary production-supporting context
  - the colony still lacks reliable metals or refinery-driven coverage
- Main risk:
  - over-duplication if another body already covers these functions
- Related live verification: `LV-0003`

## AP-0003 - Water World Tourism Orbital Pair

- Status: Conceptual
- Source: `ontology/roles_assets_strategies.md`, `mechanics/M-0001-water-worlds-have-no-surface-slots.md`
- Objective served: Exploit Water World orbital-only constraints in a tourism-oriented architecture.
- Typical roles:
  - orbital service or mission role
  - tourism-support role
- Prerequisites:
  - orbital-only planning must be respected
  - tourism objective must matter more than surface industrial flexibility
- Main risk:
  - invalid surface recommendations if body constraints are ignored
- Related live verification: future work required

## AP-0004 - HMC Industrial Ground Cluster

- Status: Conceptual
- Source: `ontology/roles_assets_strategies.md`
- Objective served: Use a High Metal Content world as a dense industrial-style ground cluster.
- Typical roles:
  - `Industrial Anchor`
  - supporting production roles
- Prerequisites:

## AP-0005 - Moonlet Weak-Link Support Chain

- Status: Conceptual but risky
- Source: `ontology/roles_assets_strategies.md`, `experiments/EXP-0000-wregoe-methodology-seed.md`
- Objective served: Use a small supporting body to influence another body's market outcomes through link behavior.
- Typical roles:
  - `Link Repair Candidate`
  - `Experiment Testbed`
- Prerequisites:
  - a clear target market problem
  - willingness to test uncertain link mechanics
- Main risk:
  - weak-link support may not become sufficient for the intended commodity recovery
- Related live verification: `LV-0003`, `LV-0005`
